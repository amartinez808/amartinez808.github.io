Meet George Mato

George Mato’s perseverance, vision, and ability to anticipate what luxury real estate buyers want—often before they realize it themselves—has fueled his extraordinary success. Guided by the belief that “great service comes from the heart,” George approaches every relationship with discretion, personal attention, and an unwavering commitment to helping clients achieve their goals.

Specializing year-round in Southwest Florida’s luxury condominium market, George blends deep market knowledge with an analytical, results-driven approach that maximizes return on investment. His reputation as a trusted advisor is built on experience, insight, decisive action, and an uncompromising standard of excellence.

Over his career, George has played a pivotal role in shaping Florida’s most prestigious communities—Fisher Island, multiple Ritz-Carlton Residences, and the Related Group’s iconic Icon Brickell Miami. He has generated more than $15 billion in development sales revenue, led teams to record-breaking sell-outs, and helped create vibrant, world-class communities.

A pioneer of Miami’s luxury condo boom in the 1990s, George helped define one of the most dynamic eras in South Florida real estate. Most recently, as Director of Sales for The Ritz-Carlton Residences, Estero Bay, he led a passionate team that achieved $700M in sales in just two years.

What truly sets George apart is his ability to build lasting relationships. He believes every client is more than a transaction—they are a partner and a friend. His guiding principle remains: “People don’t care how much you know until they know how much you care.”

For those seeking the pinnacle of luxury living in Southwest Florida, George Mato is the trusted expert who transforms aspirations into reality.
