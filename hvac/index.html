How It Works

Three simple steps. Works with RingCentral, Nextiva, Vonage, and most VoIP systems.

Step 01

Missed Call Capture

Your phone system syncs with RAD AI via webhook or call forwarding. When a call is missed after-hours or when lines are busy, our system triggers instantly.

Step 02

Instant SMS Recovery

The caller receives an immediate text message asking for the emergency details. No more losing jobs to the next company on Google.

Step 03

Auto-Dispatch

High-priority emergencies are identified and routed to your on-call technician or booking system (ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, etc.) automatically.
